Y2 Computing, 2020-21: assignment 2
-----------------------------------

This folder (assignment2) contains all the files for your second assignment:

- assignment2.ipynb:
  The is the Jupyter notebook with your tasks.
- black_box_example.ipynb:
  A simple example how to interact with the "Box"

available on Canvas: this note introduces some elements of the assignment
such as the Fourier Transform, electric circuits and transfer functions.
